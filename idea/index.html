Our Story

A Tuscan Heritage

In the sun-drenched heart of Florence, Sotto la Luna pays homage to the generations of chefs who defined Italian cuisine. Our philosophy is simple: honor the land, respect the ingredients, and share every meal with passion.

We invite you to a space where red-and-white checkers meet refined elegance, creating a sanctuary for both the weary traveler and the local gourmet.

Artisanal

Handmade pasta crafted daily with organic flour and farm-fresh eggs.

Authentic

Recipes passed down through family journals, kept secret for decades.
